About It's Showtime Inc.

25 years of premium AV excellence in Orange and LA Counties

Our Story

Founded in 1998, It's Showtime Inc. began as a small home theater installation company with a passion for bringing cinematic experiences into residential spaces. Over our 25 years in business, we've grown into Orange and LA Counties' premier provider of comprehensive audiovisual solutions.

What started with home theaters has expanded to include whole-home audio systems, sophisticated home automation, and expert TV mounting services. Our team of certified technicians has completed thousands of installations, each one customized to our clients' specific needs and spaces.
